About Kaliganj Hands

Together We Build, Together We Grow

"Kaliganj Hands is a community service organization founded with a simple belief — even a small act of kindness can bring meaningful change in people's lives."

Our Story

Started in 2020 by local friends during the pandemic. Today, we are hundreds of hands working together for Kaliganj.

Our Purpose

Bridging the gap between those who want to help and those who need it. Bringing dignity, opportunity, and hope.

Our Vision

A self-reliant Kaliganj where no child sleeps hungry and every elderly feels cared for.
